# Company Service for JobiGo

This service manages companies and job postings.

Features
- Django + DRF
- Models: Company, Category, Location, Skill, Job
- Job CRUD, public listing with filters and pagination
- Recruiter-scoped permissions for job management
- Local media storage for logos and attachments
- Dockerized with entrypoint for migrations and Gunicorn
- Demo fixtures included

This service intentionally avoids any paid external services.
